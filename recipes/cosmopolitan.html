Cosmopolitan

Cocktail that gained popularity during the 1990s from a television show.-source: allrecipes.com

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 fluid ounces vodka
  • 1/4 fluid ounce lime juice
  • 1/4 fluid ounce triple sec
  • 1/4 fluid ounce cranberry juice
  • 1 cup ice
  • 1 lime wedge for garnish

Directions:

  1. Combine vodka, lime juice, triple sec, and cranberry juice in a cocktail shaker.
  2. Add ice, cover and shake until chilled. Strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
  3. Garnish with a lime wedge.
